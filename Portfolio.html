FieldTracker Project

FieldTracker is a project I worked on with a group of NJIT graduate students for GirlHacks 2023, a hackathon hosted by NJIT. FieldTracker is a digital companion for scientific documentation with geolocation functionality to store notes, media, and audio clips within a secure database. Check out the project on Devpost for more details.
